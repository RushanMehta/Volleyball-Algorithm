Mathematical Formulation & Framework

This computational framework models volleyball sets as a discrete finite Markov chain resolved without simulation variance through backwards induction dynamic programming.

Equation 1: Serve Outcome Partition Completeness

$$\sum P(\text{Outcome}_i) = P(\text{Ace}) + P(\text{Error}) + P(\text{Poor}) + P(\text{Average}) + P(\text{Perfect}) = 1.0$$

Equation 2: Rally Win Probability Model

$$P(\text{Win Rally} \mid \text{Serve}) = P(\text{Ace}) + \sum_{s \in \text{States}} P(\text{Outcome}_s) \times (1.0 - \text{Kill}_s)$$
